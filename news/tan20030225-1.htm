Ice in Bedford TX

25 Feb 2003

There wasn't much snow, and it wasn't very thick, but a blanket of ice covered north Texas last night. We expect more tonight. I plan to try to get around in it later. The temp isn't expected to reach 30, so we could have a lot of bumper cars this week.

The PI Attorneys and physical therapists are happy with this, I imagine. Many highway interchanges are blocked by big trucks that got stuck on the ice and can't get up or down.
