About the Listserv

The email list for the Texas Rangers is hosted by Texas A&M. To subscribe, click here. If you've never been on a mailing list before, it works like this. A person sends an email to the list, and all of the subscribers get it. Each person subs and unsubs (the cool terms for signing up and leaving the list) for themselves. This list is not moderated.

About the List

It seems like I've been on this list since the new ballpark opened. I'm trying to remember the first list day. I can't remember what year that was. Anyhow, the list is a nice bunch of baseball fans, with a below average dose of stats, an above average discussion of the minor leaguers, and nowhere near enough girls.
