WHO'S HOT

Alex Rodriguez has played great this week. TV Broadcaster Tom Grieve called an ARod homer in Anaheim this week - not many players can hit it out on demand. Juan Gonzalez has homered two nights in a row, in front of Dodger scouts. - both of them turning the game around at the time.

John Thomson has pitched well both times this week. Tony Mounce has had four good outings in a row. Ron Mahay and RA Dickey are getting people out, and Brian Shouse is holding down the left side of the bullpen (Aaron Fultz is on the DL).

WHO'S NOT

Raffy is really struggling. Doug Glanville, not quite recovered from hamstring surgery, is on the bench with a 2 for 20 hangover.

Ismael Valdes has struggled in his last two outings, although he says his shoulder is fine. Joaquin Benoit had a bad game in his only start, and they will give his elbow more rest before he pitches again.
