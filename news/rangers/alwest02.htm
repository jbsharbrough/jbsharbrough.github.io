2002 American League West Pennant Race

Late in the season, with the Rangers out of it in the west, I started checking to see how they were doing against other teams. Until that pratfall at the end, they were running right in the middle of the league.

I'm sorry to say that I don't update this graph like I would if the Rangers hadn't already trashed the season. Still, a baseball season tells the story of humanity: men under pressure - traveling, competing in a very public setting against the best in the world, looking inside themselves and hoping they find something there.
