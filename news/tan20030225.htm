I'm sick of Iraq

The UN has tried sanctions, war, inspections ... and more inspections. In return, Saddam has promised to disarm, without actually doing so, for 12 years. He blames other countries for keeping the sanctions in place, claiming that the US is starving Iraqi children. He bypasses sanctions with secret deals. It's time for Saddam to get with the program. [more]

 

 

I'll get sick of North Korea fast enough

Let's see, we made a deal to provide food and other aid in exchange for them abandoning a nuclear program. They took the food and built the nukes anyway. We found out and stopped sending food. They threatened war. South Korea recommends more aid to solve the problem. We hear a lot about how we need to understand them. When is someone going to try to understand us? [more]
