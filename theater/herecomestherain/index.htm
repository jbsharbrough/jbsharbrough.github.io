Here Comes The Rain

Run dates: March 31 - April 2, 1977
Venue: Rudder Forum

In March of 1977, the Aggie Players produced a student production called "Here Comes The Rain." I was a graduating senior that semester, and was grateful that they let me direct a musical that I had written. It was first written in the summer of 1975, but like most theater, it was finished about halfway through the second night.
