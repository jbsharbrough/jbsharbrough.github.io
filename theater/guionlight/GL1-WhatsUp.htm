What's Up?

Current Aggie Player Activities

So we just finished doing "A...my name is Alice" in the Rudder Forum. It ran for two weeks, and got the attendance up to fair by the time it closed. It was the best musical that I have worked on at a&m, and that would include such classics as Celebration, Man of La Mancha, Here Comes the Rain, and, ok, that's probably all of the musicals that I worked on except the Pinchpenny Phantom of the Opera last summer. The cast consisted of 5 talented actresses and singers named stephany alvaredo, luci risinger, julie simpsoon, sarah kent, and amy e looper. the play is a feminist view of the lot in life that women have, and was directed by coach. Okay, maybe LaMancha was a better BIG production ...
