The Playbill:

News & Notes

So rumor has it that ... Tony Hall wants to have a reunion this summer. At Nancye Gandy Gorman’s house in Houston. Nancye asks that all interested parties contact Tony because if she’s providing the house he can do everything else, ok?

Aggie Players are e-mailing with the Big Guys. Walt Meissner, Bruce Monroe, Chuck Adams, and Beth Miller Loubet have all exchanged electronic missives since the first newsletter. Yes, Beth is back. I’m crazy about her. If I delete the curse words, her letter said, "If you knew my address and my husband’s name, why didn’t you just call?" Well, I knew your old address, but I had a pretty low confidence level that it was still your domicile, Beth. And I knew that you had been engaged to Mr Loubet, but the other Beth Miller said that she heard that you were divorced, and I thought ... well, we’re glad we know how to find you now. Beth is still at Origin and they are making GREAT computer games!

Bruce Monroe got a Christmas letter from Walt Meissner and replied to Walt’s E-mail address. Walt was kind enough to share it with me. Bruce is alive and well in our nation’s capital, and I’m so glad to hear it!
