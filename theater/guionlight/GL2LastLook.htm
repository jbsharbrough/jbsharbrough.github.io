LAST LOOK: recent photos of your old schoolmates.

Dan and Leah Sullins, of Arlington Texas, have donated the negatives of their Uvalde pictures. Their gift establishes the Aggie Players Library, a sort of virtual library in my garage, which presently contains a couple of playbills, some negatives, and two copies of this newsletter.

 

Some things never change.
