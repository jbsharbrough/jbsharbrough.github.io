PUSH

Written by
Beau Sharbrough
1300 Maple Ln
Provo, UT 84604
USA

PUSH is a play that I wrote in 1977.  I intended to lampoon the folly of waiting on a person in life, like waiting on a bus without knowing the schedule.  The story went another way and I just followed it.  Enjoy the story, it's not real.
