PUSH Scene 05

SCENE 5: Another film or videotape, or even a dreamlike scene behind a scrim. Ellen Faltry acts out the first two verses of "Woke Up Blue."

Woke up this morning
feeling blue
slept alone last night
guess it’s through
says who?

Got up and fixed my face
put on my hat and my shoes
ran out and got in the race
gotta beat those blues

don’t like what I’m feeling
don’t know what to do
wish I could say something
to that man about what he put me through

won’t take it lying down
won’t take it sitting in this chair
wanna make it on my own tow feet
might take it on the chin
but I’ve just gotta do something
somehow.

I’m giving warning
to everything in pants
that moves
if you’re out on the town this evening
I’m gonna dance
take a chance with you

I’m gonna spend the evening on the street
hit every bar and hotel
wish they could see me back on Avenue I
cause I’ll be loaded for bear

I’ve just gotta beat those blue
gotta bounce back right away
gotta find me someone else
maybe today.
