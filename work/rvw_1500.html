GENERAL IMPRESSION.  Harper's is a magazine that keeps me in balance.  I read a lot of science and technology publications, and I get a bit of a bias that Harper's balances out.  I never agree with Lewis Lapham and he's always right.  So I buy the magazine, read the editorials, and try to learn something.  I'll never forget the editorial about the George Bush Invasion of Panama.  I've never performed such a quick 180 in my thinking outside of an argument with a woman.
