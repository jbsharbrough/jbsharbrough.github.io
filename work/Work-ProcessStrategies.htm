Work - Process Automation Strategies

I see so many businesses where people are reading from something that they printed out of a computer, and keying the information back into a computer! All processes have some combination of moving information and moving stuff. Networks enable firms to digitize the "information flow" throughout the organization, and to suppliers and customers. Many firms have invested in networks and mail servers and web servers but are not using them to manage the flow of business processes. 

This situation puts them at a greater disadvantage every year. Better communication and followup make people and organizations more dependable, reduce errors, and speed up results.
