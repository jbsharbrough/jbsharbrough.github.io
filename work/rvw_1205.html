Discoveries, June 30, 1997

by Beau Sharbrough
The Discoveries section of the Dallas Morning News often features articles of relevance to non-linear dynamic systems.  The various sections are here identified, and where there is special interest, reviewed.


ASTRONOMY

LIFE BEYOND EARTH: Tantalizing new evidence is fueling science's search

Mars

The Mars meteorite has people wondering if there ever was life on Mars. I'm wondering if there's intelligent life on Earth. the section includes a summary speculating on the path that meteroite ALH 84001 took to get from there to here.

Europa

This Jovian moon has got a lot of water. As diverse as the environments are on Earth that contain life, it's just possible. A sidebar includes a summar pf previous planetary expeditions.

Other solar systems

We've discovered some planets around other stars. Most are uninhabitable by our standards.

ACOUSTICS

Scientists say noise levels can be major hindrance to learning. Everyone knows that you can't learn what you can't hear, but scientists have come right out and said it now. Of course, that means that classrooms are not designed properly, and that there is no easy way to dampen the noise in existing classrooms. Someday, maybe, they'll work that out.
