Dvorak

Dvorak's Fail-Safe Advice. Being ready for a major component of your personal computer system to fail. Ouch!

Intel CPU's coming slowly. Big RAM chips coming quickly. MPEG chips coming quickly. Online travel services are fighting. New OS's are muddying that water. And the crutchphrase of the week: modern bloatware. Ain't it the truth?

Fastest Desktops

Graphical Workstations are computers that handle CPU- and graphics-intensive applications such as CAD, CASE, and animation. The PC makers are increasing performance, and they have the best price/performance ratios. The Workstation makers are countering by offering less expensive models to complicate the choice of the buyer further (ok, they may have another motive in mind as well).

The editors say to be sure that you match up your needs with the product when things are so ... confusing.
