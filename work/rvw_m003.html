Spitfire Grill

Summary: This is a pretty good movie. It has a little bit of Maine in it, but they don't all talk like stereotypical Maine people.

Plot Summary: A young lady gets out of a Maine prison, and gets a job in a restaurant in Maine. She changes the entire town. The plot does not follow your normal formula.

Question: At the climax of the movie, the Girl is running to save Eli from the Posse. She doesn't get there in time. However, when the first guy from the Posse gets to Eli, he doesn't shoot him or even come close. It's almost as if she didn't have to be protecting him in the first place.

Good Line: "You can say that twice, and mean it." Apparently, they don't say, "You can say that again," in Maine.

On a five star scale, this movie goes above the middle. Three and a half stars.

Reviewed: 7/24/97
