GENERAL IMPRESSION.  The Economist is my preferred source of international news.  In addition to their coverage of the globe, the editorials seem to me to contain balance while still taking a stand - something I don't see much anymore.  The surveys that they produce are textbooks in themselves, and the primary source of information for me in almost every area that they address.

THE PARTICULARS (from the fine print).  THE ECONOMIST (ISSN 0013-0613) is published every week, except for a year-end double issue, by The Economist Newspaper, NA, Incorporated.

HOW TO FIND THEM.  Website:  www.economist.com. E-mail can be left there for a variety of departments.   Numerous articles and surveys are available on line with a subscription.
