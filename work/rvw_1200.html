GENERAL IMPRESSION.  The Discoveries page of the Dallas Morning News generally can be found on Mondays in the back of the Business section of the paper.  It might be aimed at children, but I personally enjoy it (and I doubt that Tom Siegfried's column is aimed at children).  There is not enough science and technology in the general press for me, so I make it a point to catch this page every week
