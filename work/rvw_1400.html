GENERAL IMPRESSION.  Fast Company is a publication that seems to anticipate exactly what I need to know in my work.  I like the irreverence and the seriousness that they exhibit simultaneously.  Articles from how native american councils make decisions to the management ideas of Dee Hock and John Seely Brown to studies in learning exercises make it one of the most important ones that I read.
