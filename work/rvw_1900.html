GENERAL IMPRESSION.  This is another magazine that contains articles unlike those I find elsewhere.  They are politically aware, and literary as well.  The summaries of other periodicals are worth the subscription price.

THE PARTICULARS (from the fine print).  WILSON QUARTERLY  (ISSN-0363-3276) is published quarterly by the Woodrow Wilson International Center for Scholars.

HOW TO FIND THEM.  Website:  wwics.si.edu/OUTREACH/WQ/QUARTERL.HTM. E-mail can be left there for a variety of departments.
