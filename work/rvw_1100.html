GENERAL IMPRESSION.  Bottom Line Personal is one of the most satisfying periodicals that I read, personally.  They have a number of experts with which they consult on issues ranging from investments to consumer concerns to family and health issues.  I've never read anywhere else how to pick up a puppy.
