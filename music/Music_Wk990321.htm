One sunny spring Sunday, the weather in Grapevine was nice, and I opened the back door and played this for fun.  I still listen to it because it calms me down.  It's too cyclical to be a chamber piece, it's just a few chords and a mood.

I was inspired to enjoy it by my friend Emily Braswell, who has managed to keep music in her life a lot better than I have.
