Ape 3

©1997 Beau Sharbrough

I started to write some music to be used as mood stuff for Eugene O'Neill's The Hairy Ape at Texas A&M. My schedule went south and I wasn't able to finish the work for the production. I did make a couple of pieces, though. This one is about a sunny morning on an African plain when the past and the future clashed.
