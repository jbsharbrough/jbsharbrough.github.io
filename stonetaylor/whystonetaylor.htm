Why Stone Taylor?

  • One of the fundamental reasons for using a consultant is that you don't have to pay for more consultant than you use. Whether you want to use one hour, ten hours, or five hundred hours a year, you get to choose the amount of resource you commit.

  • Experience.  I’ve worked since 1977 in manufacturing, computers, and healthcare.  I’ve programmed, managed programmers, clerked, and managed a staff of 30.  I’ve dealt with Vari*Lite, Michael’s Stores, Choice Homes, Medicare, and private physicians, attorneys, and vendors.  I’ve written software that was used for ten years afterward.  I’ve succeeded some and I’ve failed some.  I’ve found my limits.

  • Communication.  I find that many business challenges are caused by communication problems.  I try to communicate the way that I want my clients to communicate – by always asking myself what the other guy knows and how he knows it. 

  • Creativity.  I like to find new ways to do things.  I try to present new perspectives, apply new tools, and find innovative approaches to challenges.

  • Philosophy.  Not only do I have a degree in it, but it influences my approach to everything.  I’m committed to promoting the interests of my clients through creation of value, commitment to excellence, and management of risk.
